Data Entry Project Portfolio

Project: Customer Information Data Entry

Description: Implemented a comprehensive data entry initiative to update and validate customer information in the company's Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system. The primary objective was to enhance data accuracy and ensure that the CRM system reflects the most current and reliable customer data.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Entered and updated customer details, including contact information and purchase history, into the CRM system.
  • Conducted thorough data validation checks to identify and rectify discrepancies or inaccuracies in the existing data.
  • Collaborated with the customer support team to address any missing or incomplete information and ensure a comprehensive customer profile.

Tools and Technologies Used:

  • Microsoft Excel for initial data preparation and cleansing.
  • Company-specific CRM software for data entry and validation.

Achievements:

Successfully updated and validated data for over 10,000 customer records, resulting in a significant improvement in the accuracy of customer information within the CRM system. This initiative led to enhanced customer communication and more personalized interactions, contributing to improved customer satisfaction.

Challenges Overcome:

Faced challenges related to incomplete customer records and discrepancies in data formats. Implemented a systematic approach to address these issues, including regular data quality checks and collaboration with other departments to fill in missing information.

Results:

Improved data accuracy by 25% within the first three months of the project, leading to more informed decision-making and targeted marketing efforts. The updated CRM system now serves as a reliable source of customer information for the entire organization.
